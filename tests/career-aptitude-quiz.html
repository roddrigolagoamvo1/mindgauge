Career Aptitude Quiz

Eight questions about how you naturally like to work — not what you studied or where you currently work. Your result points to one of four broad directions: Builder (hands-on, technical, tangible results), Strategist (analysis, planning, systems), Helper (people, support, teaching), or Creator (original ideas, design, expression). This is a quick self-reflection prompt, not a career-placement test — treat the result as a starting point for thinking, not a verdict.

How it's measured

Each answer adds a point toward one of four directions. After eight questions, the direction with the most points is your headline result, shown alongside all four scores so you can see your full profile.

Frequently asked questions

Does this tell me my exact ideal job?

No — it points to a broad direction, not a specific job title. Real career decisions should weigh your actual skills, interests and circumstances too, ideally with a career counselor's input.

Can I be a mix of categories?

Yes — plenty of people score close across two. The bars show your full profile, not just the top label.
