Personality Type Quiz

Eight quick questions about how you typically decide things and how you typically plan your time. There's no "correct" answer — pick whichever option feels closest to how you actually behave, not how you'd like to behave. At the end, you'll get one of four archetypes — Architect, Maverick, Guardian, or Connector — plus a breakdown showing how strongly you leaned toward each of the other three, since most people are a blend rather than a pure type.

How it's measured

Each answer you pick adds a point toward one of four archetypes. After eight questions, the archetype with the most points becomes your headline result, and the result screen shows all four scores side by side so you can see your full profile, not just the top label.

The four archetypes

Architect — decides with logic, plans ahead. Maverick — decides with logic, prefers to improvise. Guardian — decides with values, plans ahead. Connector — decides with values, prefers to improvise.

Frequently asked questions

Is this based on Myers-Briggs (MBTI)?

No — this is an original, simplified four-type framework built for MindGauge around two everyday tendencies (how you decide, how you plan). It isn't a licensed or clinical instrument.

Can I be a mix of two types?

Yes, and that's common. The bars below your headline result show all four scores, so you can see how close the runner-up was.

Will I get the same result if I retake it?

Probably close, but a short quiz like this naturally has some variation depending on mood and context — treat it as a snapshot, not a permanent label.
