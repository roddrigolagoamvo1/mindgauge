Love Language Quiz

People tend to notice — and show — affection differently: some through words, some through doing things, some through simply being present, through touch, or through small thoughtful gestures. This eight-question quiz is an original, simplified self-reflection built for MindGauge; it isn't affiliated with any specific published assessment. Answer based on what actually makes you feel cared for, not what you think sounds nicest.

How it's measured

Each answer adds a point to one of five categories: Affirming Words, Helpful Actions, Focused Time, Physical Closeness, and Thoughtful Gifts. After eight questions, your strongest category is shown first, with a bar for every category so you can see your full profile rather than a single forced label.

Frequently asked questions

Is this the official "5 Love Languages" test?

No — this is an original quiz built for MindGauge around five general ways people express and notice affection. It isn't affiliated with, and doesn't reproduce, any specific published or trademarked assessment.

Can my result be a close mix of two categories?

Yes, that's common — the bars show your score across all five, not just the top one.
