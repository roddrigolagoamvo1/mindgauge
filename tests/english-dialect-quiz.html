English Dialect Quiz

English splits into some genuinely confusing local vocabulary depending where you are — a "jumper" in the UK has nothing to do with what an American calls one. This ten-question quiz tests how well you know everyday words that differ between the US, UK, Australia, and Canada. No audio here — just the words themselves, which turn out to be plenty revealing on their own.

Frequently asked questions

Why text instead of audio accents?

Accurately representing real accents needs recordings of real native speakers, which this quiz doesn't use. Vocabulary differences are just as revealing and work reliably in any browser.

Are these words used everywhere in that country?

Not necessarily — usage varies by region, generation, and context even within one country. These are commonly recognized examples, not strict rules.
